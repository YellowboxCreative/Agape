Home Features

To make these homes unique, beautiful, and easy to resell in the future, designers have created three different base designs. These designs are: Warm, Gray, or White. Once you pick your base design you will make a few selections within each base design, like color of cabinets, and countertop color and material. All these choices are included in the price of the home and will not cost you more or less based on your choice.

Warm: Wall color is Agreeable Gray.
Gray: Wall color is Repose Gray.
White: Wall color is First Star.

All base designs include a choice of:

Light or Dark Cabinets
Quartz or Granite counter tops
Vinyl in the main rooms
Carpet in the bedrooms

All homes include
Frigidaire Stainless Steel Appliances:

30” Freestanding Range
1.8 Cu. Ft. Over-the-Range Microwave
24” Built-in Dishwasher
